# HappyDiary_Android_TeamProject
## Introduction
The Happy Diary is a journal app that helps users record their daily life. Users can preserve their precious moments with texts and photos. For an exclusive user experience, the app offers a variety of helpful functions to maximize customization, from personalized stickers to daily mood documentation. The Happy Diary essentially serves as a digital time capsule for users to keep track of those ups and downs that happened in life; as time continues to flow by, you will have something to hold on to.
